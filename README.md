This dashboard shows the number of complaints in nutrition clinics at healthcare centers in Saudi during  2024 years 
# Analysis of Nutrition Clinic Appointments and Complaints

## 🎯 Project Objective
The primary objective of this Power BI dashboard is to analyze, monitor, and evaluate patient appointments and complaint cases within nutrition clinics across various healthcare centers and hospitals. By examining the nature of complaints and their geographic distribution, this project aims to support healthcare stakeholders in optimizing clinic performance, enhancing patient satisfaction, and strategic resource allocation based on critical case density.

## 📊 Data Source
The dataset utilized for this project was securely provided by my Internship Training Supervisor during my clinical nutrition internship period. To maintain strict data privacy and professional ethics, all sensitive institutional details and patient identifiers have been completely anonymized, while preserving the analytical integrity required for statistical evaluation.

## 💡 Key Insights & Findings (Based on 2024 Data Analysis)
An aggregate analysis of the dashboard reveals several critical findings regarding the performance and challenges facing the nutrition clinics:


* **Total Volume of Cases:** During the analyzed period of 2024, a total of **365 complaint cases** were registered across the designated clinics.
* **Severity Classification:** 
  * **Critical Cases:** Represent the majority with **208 cases**, indicating a pressing need for immediate intervention and clinical review in specific regions.
  * **Non-Critical Cases:** Account for **157 cases** of the total volume.
* **Complaint Categories:** Patient feedback is categorized into two main types:
  * **Clinic Complaints (clinic compl...):** By far the largest segment with **188 cases**, heavily dominating the pie chart distribution. This highlights that operational or service-related aspects within the clinics require the most administrative attention.
  * **Formula Complaints (formula comp...):** Comprise a smaller subset of **20 cases**, related to nutritional formulas or product provisions.
* **Geographical Distribution & Regional Load:** 
  * The stacked bar chart **"Distribution of Critical Cases in Hospitals"** and the accompanying **Geographic Map** demonstrate a significant concentration of complaints and critical loads in major regional hubs (such as Riyadh, Makkah, and Eastern Region centers).
  * Certain hospitals display a disproportionately high density of combined critical cases and clinic complaints, signaling specific locations where resource optimization or staff training should be prioritized.
